THE GREAT SSI QUEST

Self-Sovereign Identity is the principle where all identity data about a user (called attestations or credentials) is under their own control.

SSI compliant Wallets, then enable the secure sharing of all or just part of that data (called Selective Disclosure) with any requesting party.

To demonstrate this in practice we at the SAFBC have built a little game that will guide to through a few of the many possible interactions to which digital identity will have a profound impact in our lives in years to come.

In this first stage of your journey, we will issue you a BAC_ID credential.
This makes you a new citizen of The Republic of Blockchania!

This credential could represent a digital version of any form of identification you have been issued before.
For example it could be an ID, a Professional License, a Company ID or even a gym membership.

Once you have completed your quest to collect the 3 other attestations from our exhibition partners, you can verify and claim your REWARD here.
