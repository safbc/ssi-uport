The South African Financial Blockchain Consortium (SAFBC) aims to demonstrate the transformative potential of blockchain technology for the South African financial industry. The consortium aims to educate and to bring the benefits of blockchain technology to the industry for the benefit of the entire country. Our membership is comprised of banks, financial markets infrastructure players and regulators as well as other institutions involved in the financial services industry.

The SAFBC is proud to be driving the awareness and adoption of Self Sovereign Identity (SSI) in South Africa. SSI enables claims, proofs, and attestations where the sharing of data is safer for both the identity provider and recipient.
